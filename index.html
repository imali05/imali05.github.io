About Me

Who I Am

I'm Imali Punsara Dissanayaka, a detail-oriented third-year undergraduate studying Applied Data Science & Communication at General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University (KDU), Ratmalana.

I specialise in turning raw, messy data into meaningful insights and visual stories. As a Data Analyst and BI Developer, I build interactive dashboards, predictive models, and automated reports that help organisations make smarter, data-driven decisions.

My toolkit spans SQL, Python, Power BI, R, and machine learning — and I'm passionate about applying these skills to real-world problems in sustainability, public health, and policy analysis.
